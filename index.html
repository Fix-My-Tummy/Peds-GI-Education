★ Why July 5th instead of the 4th?

The 4th belongs to the municipal shows — Gulfport Harbor, the Shuckers game, Ocean Springs waterfront. Go enjoy 'em. Then come to The Hill on the 5th, where there's no crowd, no parking lot, and the finale answers to nobody.

★ Is this a fancy event?

Life's a garden — dig it. Come as you are: camo, cutoffs, or Sunday best. Mullets receive priority parking.

★ Kids and dogs?

Both welcome. Snakes and sparklers for the young'uns — it's the only way they learn. Dogs who take personal offense at loud noises may prefer to sit this one out.

★ What kind of fireworks?

Aerial shells out of HDPE mortar tubes — chrysanthemums, crackle, whistlers, mines & tails, and reports you'll feel in your chest. Four rounds, loaded and sequenced in daylight like professionals (ish). Your only job: vote for the best one (see below).

★ What if the weather's bad?

Gulf Coast July: hot, humid, 30% chance of drama. We'll make the call by noon on the 5th. The shells keep — the shrimp doesn't.

Judge the Show
★ Four Rounds. One Champion. ★

The crates get loaded and sequenced in daylight — the menu's locked long before dark. Your job comes after the smoke clears: crown the round that hit hardest. One vote per person. Choose wisely — no take-backsies on The Hill.
